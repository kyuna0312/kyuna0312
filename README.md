## Story

Solo developer and open-source builder. I care about **clear interfaces**, **boring‑until‑it‑works infrastructure**, and **playful, feminine, anime‑coded aesthetics** without apologizing for either side.

- **Software:** web products, APIs, and the occasional dive into OS, kernels, and low-level toys when curiosity wins.
- **Open source:** configs, small tools, and experiments I want others to fork, break, and improve—issues and PRs welcome.
- **AI & comics:** pipelines and workflows for AI‑assisted art and comics; treating models as part of the stack, not magic.
- **Content:** long-form on YouTube, day-to-day on X/Instagram—same narrative as the repos, just a different surface.

## Open source & collaboration

- **Good first issues:** I tag approachable work when I can—watch repos you care about.
- **PRs:** smaller diffs with a clear “why” beat giant rewrites; I review as fast as life allows.
- **Sponsorship / shoutouts:** if you build on something here, @ me—I like seeing forks in the wild.

If you’re a **recruiter**: start with [my site](https://kyuna-web.vercel.app/) and the **featured repos** below; they’re the most representative.
